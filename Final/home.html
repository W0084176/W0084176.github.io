About Me

I am originally, from Granby, Quebec which is a city 1 hour outside of Montreal. When I was living in Quebec, I actually went to a completely french school therefore am bilingual.

In 1999, my family decided to move to Bridgetown Nova Scotia which is a small town in the Annapolis valley. That is where I finished my high school education. When I graduated, that is when I decided to go to college and study Information Technology for the first time. So a few years later I graduated and since then been working various jobs.

In the spring of 2017, I ended being laid off from my construction job and thats when I decided to go back to school and pursue IT for the second time.
